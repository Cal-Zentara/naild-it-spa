Our Story

It started long before the salon.

Before Nail'd It! Spa, there was a kitchen table and a mother who made every client feel beautiful. That's where Dalena learned — and that's the standard she's kept ever since.

Today, that same love and attention lives in every service we offer. We don't just do nails — we create an experience that leaves you feeling cared for, confident, and ready to show off.

Longer-lasting results, honest pricing, and a team that genuinely loves what they do.
