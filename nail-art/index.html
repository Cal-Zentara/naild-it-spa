Questions

Nail Art FAQ

How much does nail art cost at Nail'd It!?

Nail art starts at $5 per nail and goes up based on design complexity. Simple accents stay on the low end. Full-set custom designs are priced per session — ask when you book and we'll quote you before we start.

Can I bring in a photo for a custom design?

Yes — please do. Bring a photo or show us on your phone. The more specific you are about what you want, the better we can match it. Screenshots from Instagram or Pinterest work great.

What type of nails can nail art be done on?

Nail art works on Gel-X, acrylic, dip/SNS, and natural nails. The canvas doesn't limit what we can do — the design is what matters.

How long does nail art add to my appointment?

Budget an extra 15–45 minutes on top of your base service. Simple accent nails on a few fingers add less time. Full custom sets on all nails take longer. Let us know what you're thinking when you book so we schedule the right amount of time.

Do you do 3D nail art?

Some 3D designs are available depending on the specific look. Reach out before booking and let us know what you have in mind — we'll tell you whether it's doable and what it would take.

Are seasonal designs available?

Yes — we do seasonal and holiday nail art all year. Valentine's Day, summer themes, Halloween, Christmas — just ask. If you have something specific in mind, bring a reference image.
