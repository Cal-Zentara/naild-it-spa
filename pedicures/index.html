Questions

Pedicure FAQ

How much does a pedicure cost at Nail'd It!?

Pedicures start at $35 for the Classic, $45 for the Happy, and $65 for the VIP. Gel polish upgrades are available on any tier — ask when you book.

What's the difference between Classic, Happy, and VIP?

Classic covers the essentials — soak, trim, shape, cuticle care, basic massage, and polish. Happy adds an extended massage and a scrub treatment. VIP goes further with hot stone massage and paraffin wax. The difference is how much time and attention you want.

Do you offer gel pedicures?

Yes — gel polish is available as an upgrade on any pedicure. It lasts longer and has a glossy finish that holds up well on toes. Just ask when booking.

How long does a pedicure take?

Classic runs about 45 minutes. Happy is closer to 60. VIP is 75–90 minutes. If you're also getting a manicure, plan for extra time — let us know when you book.

Do you take walk-ins for pedicures?

Yes, walk-ins are welcome every day of the week. Weekends and afternoon slots fill up fastest — booking ahead guarantees your preferred time.

Can I get a pedicure and manicure together?

Absolutely — combination appointments are one of the most common requests. Just mention it when booking online or by phone and we'll make sure there's enough time for both.
