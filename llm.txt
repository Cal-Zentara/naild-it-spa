# Nail'd It! Spa

Nail'd It! Spa is a full-service nail salon in Stanton, CA serving Orange County. The salon specializes in Gel-X, acrylic, dip/SNS, pedicures, and nail art. Known for quality work, a welcoming atmosphere, and a team with deep roots in the nail care industry.

## About
Nail'd It! Spa is owned by Dalena Huynh, a Vietnamese-American nail technician whose mother was also in the nail care industry — making this a family tradition passed down through generations. Dalena and her team bring genuine craft and care to every client, building a loyal following across Stanton, Anaheim, Garden Grove, Westminster, and surrounding OC cities.
