What Is Gel-X?

Gel-X is a soft gel extension system — pre-shaped tips bonded to your natural nail with gel adhesive, then cured under an LED lamp. No acrylic powder, no harsh liquid monomer, no strong smell.

The result is a flexible, natural-feeling nail that still holds its shape. It's stronger than regular polish but gentler than traditional acrylics. Ideal for clients who want real length without the wear and tear on their natural nail.

A full set lasts 3–4 weeks. Removal is a simple acetate soak — no filing, no damage. Prefer acrylic? We do those too.

Common Questions

Gel-X FAQ

What is Gel-X and how is it different from acrylic?

Gel-X uses pre-shaped soft gel tips applied with gel adhesive and cured under an LED lamp. No acrylic powder or monomer liquid — so no chemical smell. The result is a lighter, more flexible nail compared to traditional acrylics.

How long do Gel-X nails last?

3–4 weeks with proper care. Using cuticle oil daily and avoiding prolonged water exposure helps them last longer. Most clients come back every 3.5 weeks for a fresh set.

Do Gel-X nails damage your natural nails?

Minimal damage compared to acrylics. There's no aggressive filing involved, and removal is done with an acetate soak. Your natural nail stays intact underneath the extension.
