How long do dip powder nails last?

Dip powder nails typically last 3 to 4 weeks before needing a fill or refresh. How long they last depends on how fast your nails grow and what you put your hands through.

What's the difference between dip powder and gel nails?

Dip powder uses a colored powder and bonding liquid — no UV lamp needed. Gel uses a gel polish cured under UV. Dip tends to feel thicker and more natural; gel is thinner and more flexible. Both last well, but dip is generally the lower-maintenance option.
